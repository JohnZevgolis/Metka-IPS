About eneron

ENERON is an innovative, easily-scalable, user-friendly uninterruptible power supply (UPS) system developed specially to support substations. The system can supply up to several thousand kilowatts of AC and DC loads for a wide range of commercial and industrial applications, such as: power generation, transmission, and distribution; oil and gas extraction; automation control; maritime; railway; and various others.

How IT works

The system relies on two main components:

  • A state-of-the-art UL3000D rectifier module that utilizes hot-swap tech and system redundancy with a superior peak efficiency reaching up to 98.6%

  • An application-oriented 14000B invertor that is compatible with multi-voltage input levels and able to produce pure sine wave AC output when needed

Tech specs

Rectifier UL3000D

The rectifier module UL3000D has Superior peak efficiency up to 98.6% and high power density, providing a low total cost of ownership. Using hot swap technology and control system redundancy, maintenance has never been easier and more cost-effective.
