Africa Energy Forum: Mini & Off Grid Summit

8 February 2018, Uganda

The 2nd Africa Energy Forum: Off the Grid meeting will focus on issues concerning the rolling out of off-grid projects across Africa, present the current project opportunities and build on the outcomes of the first successful Off-Grid Summit in December 2016.

The event will take place in Kampala, Uganda, during 6-8 February 2018, with METKA among the main sponsors, in line with its business development strategy toward hybrid-off grid power solutions.
