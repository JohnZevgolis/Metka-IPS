EXERON Features

X is Al-in-One modular power conversion System initially developed by IPS for defence applications

Tech Specs

X incorporates solar change controllers, DG/Grid rectifiers and inverters under the control of a single Monitoring and Control Unit (MCU) - the X’s brain.

Benefits

The beauty of EXERON - the modular structure and the host pluggable modules make the installation like child’s play.

Exchange of modules or power upgrade is easily done within a few seconds without any tools or special skills. The X remains powered and operational during module exchange or upgrade.
